BAKED MAC AND CHEESE

Baked mac and cheese. It’s nearly the universal comfort food, and there are SO many variations! In fact, I almost didn’t post this recipe, as there are SOOOO many mac and cheese recipes available, and most people already have their favorite way of making it. Of course, there’s no “right” way or “wrong” way, so I’m here sharing the way I like to make my mac and cheese (the way my family loves it too).

Ingredients

  • pasta shells
  • cheese
  • milk
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. boil pasta
  2. add cheese
  3. bake for 30 mins on 350
  4. Enjoy!
